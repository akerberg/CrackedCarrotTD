Multiplayer

When you're done playing the singleplayer-game and think there is nothing more anyone can teach you it's time to step it up a bit and take on the most challenging prey of all - man.

How to play:
When you're playing multiplayer the game is a little bit different than the standard gameplay. Now you're competing against your friends to get the highest score while trying to lose the least amount of carrots. To aid you in this you have a number of new abilities you can purchase and punish your opponent with.

Special abilities:
  • Carrot Lover
    • Steal some of your opponent's carrots. Cannot be used to win the game. Costs 100 gold.
  • Teardown Tower
    • Destroys a random opponent tower. A favourite among the developers. Costs 200 gold.
  • Shield
    • Protects you against one incoming special ability. Costs 400 gold.
  • Enraged Critters
    • The critters attacking your opponent gains unnatural abilities, turning them into horrible adversaries. Costs 200 gold.

    Pairing:
    In order to successfully connect to each other both phones must have Bluetooth enabled and be paired up before starting the game. Then one player hosts the game and the other and the other joins.
    If there are problems connecting or maintaining connection please try moving closer together or switching who is hosting and who is joining.

    Hints:
  • Think about how you build your towers and spend your money. If you both kill the same amount of critters the interest will tip the scale...
  • Some of the new abilities are good all-around, others have their special purpose.
  • When playing multiplayer the waves change a bit compared to normal play...
