Creatures


During your battle againts the forces of evil you will face many different kind of enemies. As the game goes on the enemies will be harder to defeat. You have to spend your money wisely to be able to defeat all levels.

To make it even harder for you protect your precious carrots the enemies have used there black magic to gaine different abilities:

Fast: These creatures will move accross the maps in twice the speed as ordinary creatures.

Fire resistant: Will only take 40% of the damage from towers with fire damage.

Frost resistant: Can't be slowed down by frost towers.

Poison resistant: Will not be affected by damage over time.

Boss: Can take more damage than avarage enemies.


Important levels:
Some levels will be harder than the rest. Here are some important levels to remember.

Level 6: First fast level.

Level 7: First posion resistant level.

Level 8: First fire resistant level.

Level 14: First frost resistant level.

Level > 14: Enemies will can have more than one ability.

Level 10,20,30: BOSS LEVEL!
