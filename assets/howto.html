How to play

The goal of this game is to try to stop enemies from crossing the map by building towers which shoot at them as they pass.

The health of the enemies will increase each level. When an enemy is defeated, the player earns money.

Money can then be used to buy or upgrade towers. If a enemy reaches the goal the player will loose one life.

When all lifes has been lost or the player have defeted all waves the game ends.
